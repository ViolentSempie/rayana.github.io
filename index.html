Bazza Bar & Comedoria

Bazza Bar & Comedoria is a sophisticated restaurant, recognized by VEJA Magazine in 2012 as Recife's best new restaurant. The place is ideal for those who enjoy the best of contemporary cuisine combined with great music, a specially prepared menu and carefully designed ambience.

In this project I was responsible for the concept and digital art direction. I put special emphasis on photography to convey the detail and sophistication of the restaurant.

SBT Nordeste: São João da Tradição

SBT Northeast, Brazil's second biggest television netwowrk paid tribute to the centenary of Luiz Gonzaga: a musician and poet, and one of most influential figures of Brazilian popular music in the twentieth century. Inspired by the poetry sung by the "King of Baião" and typical elements of the northeastern tradition, the website tells the history of Gonzaga's life through the use of illustrations and typical of bonfire elements of St. John's Day celebrations in the Northeast Region of Brazil .

The website uses parallax scrolling and different backgrounds, which is perfect for storytelling. I was responsible for the Art Direction and Design of this project.
